Tic Tac Toe Game

Click "Start Game" to begin

Addendum: In the JavaScript file, I implemented a Tic-Tac-Toe game. Players take turns clicking on the cells to place their marks ('X' or 'O'). The game checks after each move if a player has won. If all cells are filled and no winner is found, the game ends in a tie. The "Start Game" button initiates the game, and the "Reset Game" button restarts it.

Difficulty and Solution: One difficulty I encountered was correctly detecting winning combinations and resetting the game state. To address this, I refined the win-checking algorithm to accurately identify winning rows, columns, and diagonals. Additionally, I improved the game reset functionality to clear the game board, reset the player turn, and update the UI accordingly.
